About Me

Neha Bathe



Hi! I am a photographer and illustrator based in India. Here you can see some of my favourite works (which is mostly inspired by my Great Heritage).
I have always been really proud of my ROOTS. I always considered myself lucky to be born in a country with such rich history. India is one of the oldest civilizations which went through innumerable changes through the ages that now the depth of history cannot even be compared with the ocean. Bharat or BharatVarsha or Hindustan is a land of diversity where everything is extreme. Mark Twain has rightly said:-
"India is the One land that all men desire to see, and having seen once, by even a glimpse, would not give that glimpse for all the shows of all the rest of the globe combined.”
This is all about my love for my country and nothing about me (LOL). Feel free to snoop around here and my social media for more of my works. For inquires about my work CONTACT ME. I will be happy to hear from you, feedback on my work ;).
